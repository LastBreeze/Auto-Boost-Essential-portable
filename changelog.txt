v1.3
---------------------------------
• Upgraded svt-av1-essential to SVT-AV1-Essential_v3.1.2-Essential_+_ffms2_v3_PSYfeat2.patch.
  - znver2 is the default binary, if your Intel or AMD CPU supports this, it will be fastest. A x86-64-v3 .exe is provided for compatibility, located in
	tools\SVT-AV1-Essential builds, you may manually switch out the .exe file if needed.
• Add resume support. Auto-Boost-Essential script supports resuming. The av1 encoding performed by svt-av1-essential does not support resuming.
• Added tool extras\compress-folders.bat. For Windows 10/11 this will compress the VapourSynth and tools folders, saving about 60% of space.
• New Troubleshooting Tool: Added extras\lossless-intermediary.bat.
  - Use this if you have a "problematic" video that crashes or refuses to encode properly.
  - It converts your video into a clean, lossless file that you can use as a stable source to ensure smooth encoding. Place your mkv file in the "tools" folder and
	run lossless-intermediary.bat
• New Opus Audio Tool: Added extras\encode-opus.audio.bat for easy audio compression.
  - Automatically extracts audio from your MKV videos and converts it to high-quality, space-saving Opus format.
  - Smartly processes multiple files at once using your CPU's full threads and saves a new video with opus audio to an output folder.
• extras\forced-aspect-remux.bat added. After av1 encoding is complete, run this to copy over forced aspect ratio from source files to the av1 mkv.
